The Classics

Check out these awesome fun facts about Disney Movies!

  • • The iconic song "Part of Your World," was almost cut from The Little Mermaid
  • • Aladdin's face was modeled after Tom Cruise's
  • • Ariel was partially modeled after Alyssa Milano
  • • The Black Cauldron was Disney's first animated film to get a PG rating
  • • Contrary to popular belief, Tinkerbell is not based on Marilyn Monroe, but on reference model Margaret Kerry
  • • Alicia Keys actually auditioned three times for the role of Princess Tiana
  • • Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs was the first film to ever release a motion-picture soundtrack
  • • One minute of The Nightmare Before Christmas required an entire week of filming
  • • Pumbaa is the first Disney character to ever fart
  • • Christian Bale voiced the character of Thomas in Pocahontas
  • • Frozen's co-director, Jennifer Lee, is the first female to direct a Disney animated movie
  • • Producers originally wanted Sean Connery to voice Mufasa
  • • The voice of Eeyore is also the voice of Transformers' Optimus Prime
